Student Dashboard Insights

Science (78.1%) and Gujarati (76.9%) are the strongest subjects, while English (72.8%) and Mathematics (73.0%) need the most attention.

Over 52% of doubts remain unresolved, which could directly affect student confidence and scores.

Doubts are concentrated in Gujarati writing and Science topics like Pollution and Electricity.

Students with better attendance consistently score higher and raise fewer doubts.

Low attendance students show weaker test performance and more doubts, highlighting attendance as a key success factor.

High-performing students are regular in attendance and require less doubt support, showing strong concept clarity.

Immediate action is required in English & Math support and improving doubt resolution to uplift overall performance.

Monitoring low-attendance students and providing targeted help will improve class performance.
